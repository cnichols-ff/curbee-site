Our Platform

M.A.R.S. is Curbee’s end-to-end platform to operationalize mobile service.

M.A.R.S. brings together booking, routing, technician execution, visibility, and communication into one connected system. Born out of real world experience, at its core, M.A.R.S. is about doing one thing really well: Sending the right van to the right job, using the right route, with the right parts, at the right time.
Book a Demo

Smart Booking & Routing

Smart Booking and Routing captures the right details upfront, then intelligently groups jobs by location, technician skill sets, service type, and traffic performance to maximize density and minimize drive time from day one.

Customer Communication

Automated updates keep customers informed at every step and can integrate with your call management software, reducing inbound calls and improving the overall service experience.

Service Operations

Real-time visibility across routes, technicians, and appointments. Live maps and status updates help teams spot issues early, adjust on the fly, and keep the day running smoothly.

Technician App

Designed for technicians on the move, the app surfaces schedules, job details, and routes in a simple, easy-to-navigate interface, using swipe-to-continue and push-to-proceed actions that keep work moving without slowing you down.

Integrations

M.A.R.S. integrates with dealership systems and OEM programs to keep data flowing where it belongs. Native integrations with Tekion and CDK, with support for Dealertrack, Reynolds & Reynolds, as well as call management and payment platforms.
